Cafe Favorite combines a comfortable, modern environment with a diverse offering of classic favorites and impeccable food service. Cafe Favorite is known for it's world-renowned unique atmosphere. Established in New Orleans, La in 1990, it has continued to thrive ever since. Through the years, Cafe Favorite has become a standard for quality of food and service. New Orleans is known for its amazing twist on classic cuisine dishes. We pride ourselves on combining spices to exemplify flavors. We are located in the heart of New Orleans and its easy to find us right on Bourbon Street. We love our customers and understand we wouldn't be here without them.
